What we Do

manna resource development center aka(MRDC) is a christian faith based, community focused development organisation. it also is a response to the state of hopelessness,poverty,ignorance and apathy being experienced in our communities. it is involved in the identification, sourcing and utilization of natural resources for economic empowerment and development of communities where these resources are found. concieved in the year 2002,it was birthed in april 2004, registered with plateau state ministry of youths and sports in the same year n got certificate of incoporation from the corporate affairs commission, abuja on the 31st october 2005 with CAC No.19762. mrdc is a christian faith base community focused development organisation. it is non-government and non-profit oriented. its scope of coverge for now is nigeria and later worldwide. it has a board of trustees, advisory team and management team. it is a member of: AONN,CRUDAN,CiSHAN, Interfaith, Research Network, NYNETHA. Amongst others it collaborates with national and international organisation
